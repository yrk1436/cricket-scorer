Design choices (from research)

  • Inverted pyramid — score + overs visible in the first glance; details below (cricket.com pattern).
  • Card-based sections — hero score, crease, last balls, scoring pad as separate glass cards (CricHeroes-style clarity).
  • Large circular run buttons — one-hand scoring; extras/wicket as labeled chips.
  • Colored ball orbits — runs green, extras amber, wickets red, strike-swap blue.
  • Dark pitch theme — reduces glare outdoors; high contrast for numbers (mono font).
